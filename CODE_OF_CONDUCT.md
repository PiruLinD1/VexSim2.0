# Code of Conduct

VexSim 2.0 should be a welcoming project for students, hobbyists, teams, and contributors of any experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially with new contributors.
- Give feedback on the code, not the person.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the simulator.
- Help make issues and pull requests clear, testable, and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally harmful contributions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or community.

If you need to report a problem, contact a maintainer privately when possible. Reports should be handled with care and kept as confidential as practical.
